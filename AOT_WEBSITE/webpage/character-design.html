Attack on Titan

進撃の巨人

CHARACTERS

Eren Yeager

三浦 春馬

Actor: Haruma Miura

A brave and rebellious young man who dreams of exploring the world beyond the walls. Traumatized by the Titan attack that destroyed his home, Eren joins the military to fight back. He later discovers he can transform into a Titan himself, making him a key figure in humanity's battle.

Mikasa Ackerman

水原 希子

Actress: Kiko Mizuhara

Eren’s childhood friend who is believed to be dead during the initial Titan invasion, only to return years later as a cold and elite soldier under Captain Shikishima. Quiet and stoic, Mikasa is a deadly fighter with a tragic past and conflicted loyalties.

Armin Arlert

本郷 奏多

Actress: Kanata Hongo

Eren’s intelligent and loyal friend who, despite lacking combat skills, provides strategic insight and technical expertise. Armin supports Eren’s ideals but often struggles with fear and self-doubt.

Captain Shikishima

長谷川 博己

Actress: Hiroki Hasegawa

A powerful and charismatic warrior who serves as a substitute for Levi in the live-action films. He becomes both a mentor and ideological rival to Eren, advocating rebellion against the corrupt government and revealing dark secrets about the Titans.

Hans (Hanji Zoe)

石原 さとみ

Actress: Satomi Ishihara

An eccentric and passionate weapons researcher obsessed with understanding Titans. Hans provides comic relief but is also crucial to the group’s survival with her technological innovations.

Kubal

國村 隼

Actress: Jun Kunimura

A high-ranking military officer with a stern and commanding presence. Kubal enforces strict control and hides the truth about the origins of Titans. He becomes a symbol of authoritarianism in the story.

Sannagi

松尾 諭

Actress: Satoru Matsuo

A quiet, strong soldier skilled in hand-to-hand combat and heavy weaponry. Loyal and selfless, Sannagi often protects others at the cost of his own safety.

Sasha

桜庭 ななみ

Actress: Nanami Sakuraba

Sasha is one of the trainees within the scout/military forces, known for her agility and fighting skills. She appears as part of the soldier squad but lacks her signature food-loving personality and backstory.

Jean Kirschtein

三浦 貴大

Actress: Takahiro Miura

A soldier with a pragmatic outlook. Often clashes with Eren’s idealism but gradually learns the value of trust and teamwork during battle.

Lil

武田 梨奈

Actress: Rina Takeda

A skilled and determined female soldier who shows strong leadership among the ranks. She represents the everyday bravery of those who fight for survival.

Fukushi

渡部 秀

Actress: Shu Watanabe

A hot-headed soldier and one of Eren’s squadmates. He often acts impulsively and struggles with fear, but remains committed to the mission.

Souda

ピエール瀧

Actress: Pierre Taki

A former government technician who knows the truth behind the Titans and the wall. He becomes a key figure in revealing hidden knowledge to Eren.
